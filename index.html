About Me

I am a permanent researcher (chargé de recherche) at Inria Rennes in the CAPSULE team. I mainly study quantum algorithms in the context of cryptanalysis. I am particularly interested in problems related to lattice-based cryptography. I am also interested in broader topics such as theoretical computer science or computational complexity.

Previously, I was a research fellow (EPSRC Fellow) at King's College London. Before that, I was a post-doc then a research fellow at Royal Holloway, University of London, hosted by Pr Martin R. Albrecht. Prior to that, I did my PhD at IRIF, Université Paris Cité under the supervision of Frédéric Magniez.

I have funding for one PhD student, one postdoc (two years) and several Master internships. Please contact me at my new email address: yixin.shen at inria.fr.
